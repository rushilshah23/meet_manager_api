Assignment
Create a Backend application in NodeJs which will help its employee to book a meeting room as and when required. 

Requirements 
Get all available slots for a specific day
Schedule meeting in the given slot.
Reschedule meeting to a given slot.
Cancel meeting if the meeting has not started
Bulk update all meetings
Bulk cancel all meetings

Preferred Tech Stack 
Backend: NodeJs
Database: MongoDB[Optional]

Note
You can assume data to suit your requirements
You should not allow overlapping meeting
You can assume start and end time of a day(e.g. 09:00 - 18:00)
